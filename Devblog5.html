The Creation of DeathBot, and its role in deathrun


Mass Units
January 30th 2024

The Birth of Dev Blogs and Dev Blog Page

It all started with the idea of keeping a changelog to document updates and changes in Brickadia Deathrun. As the game evolved, so did our ambition to provide transparency and communication with our players. We decided to expand into dev blogs, sharing development insights and community updates.

Creating the Dev Blog Page: We crafted a dedicated page on our website to host our dev blogs. This page serves as a central hub for sharing behind-the-scenes details and progress reports.

Dev Blogs Themselves: Our dev blogs provide an inside look into the game's development journey, from map design to new gameplay mechanics. We aim to keep our community informed and engaged through regular updates.

The Changelog

We recognized the importance of maintaining a detailed changelog to keep everyone informed about the latest updates and changes in Brickadia Deathrun. This changelog serves as a historical record of our development progress and improvements.

Building the Support Page

The Support page was a critical addition to our website. We wanted to provide players with direct channels to report issues and apply for staff roles.

  • Player Report Form: Report rule violations or disruptive behavior encountered in-game to help maintain fair gameplay.
  • Bug Report Form: Notify us of technical issues or gameplay glitches for prompt resolution.
  • Staff Application Form: Interested in joining our team? Apply to become a staff member and contribute to Brickadia Deathrun's growth.

Conclusion

The creation of our HTML website for Brickadia Deathrun was driven by a passion for community engagement and transparency. Starting with a simple changelog, we expanded our vision to build a comprehensive platform that enhances the player experience. We look forward to evolving and improving our website as we continue this incredible journey together!
