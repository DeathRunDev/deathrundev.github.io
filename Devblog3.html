DevBlog #3- Creating the first Deathrun maps in Brickadia

What should Brickadia Deathrun maps be like? And Developing Neon Highlights and Soviergn heights.

Red
January 30th 2024

Introduction

In this blog post, we'll delve into the development process behind the creation of the first two deathrun maps in Brickadia: "Sovereign Heights" and "Neon Highlights". We'll discuss how we discovered the requirements for these maps and the design considerations that shaped their development.


Discovery of Requirements

• Concept Exploration: Exploring the theme of city pursuit and neon obstacle courses.

• Player Engagement: Understanding what engages players in deathrun maps.

• Technical Feasibility: Considering the game engine's capabilities and constraints.


Development of "Sovereign Heights"

• Concept and Storyline: Pursuit through city rooftops, streets, and sewers.

• Trap Design: Incorporating city-themed traps like helicopters, collapsing buildings, and street obstacles.

• Visual Design: Creating an urban environment with detailed textures and realistic elements.

• Gameplay Testing: Iterative testing to refine trap timings and player experience.


Development of "Neon Highlights"

• Concept and Design: Neon-highlighted obstacle course with platforming challenges.

• Artistic Direction: Choosing vibrant colors and futuristic aesthetics.

• Obstacle Variety: Incorporating moving platforms, jump puzzles, and timed obstacles.

• Player Interaction: Ensuring smooth gameplay flow and challenge balance.


Additional Design Insights

• Player Feedback: Integrating community input during development.

• Iterative Improvement: How we refined and polished the maps over time.

• Collaborative Process: Highlighting the teamwork involved in map creation.

• Technical Challenges: Overcoming hurdles in implementing specific traps or mechanics.


Conclusion

In conclusion, "Sovereign Heights" and "Neon Highlights" represent our initial foray into map design for Brickadia Deathrun. These maps not only challenge players but also showcase our commitment to innovative and engaging level design.


Call to Action

Stay tuned for more behind-the-scenes insights and future updates on Brickadia Deathrun map development!


Future Map Requirements

As we continue to expand the world of Brickadia Deathrun, we're constantly evaluating and refining our approach to map design.

• Diverse Themes: Exploring a range of themes, from urban environments to exotic locales, to keep the gameplay experience fresh and exciting.

• Interactive Elements: Introducing more interactive elements such as dynamic traps, hidden passages, and interactive scenery to enhance player engagement.

• Community Engagement: Incorporating feedback and suggestions from the community to ensure that our maps resonate with players and meet their expectations.

• Challenge Balance: Striking the right balance between difficulty and accessibility, catering to both seasoned players seeking a challenge and newcomers looking for fun.

• Visual Appeal: Elevating visual aesthetics with attention to detail, immersive environments, and thematic consistency across all maps.

• Innovative Mechanics: Experimenting with new gameplay mechanics and features to push the boundaries of what's possible within the Brickadia Deathrun universe.

• Performance Optimization: Ensuring smooth performance across a range of hardware configurations, prioritizing gameplay fluidity without compromising on visual quality.


Looking Ahead

As we look ahead to future updates and expansions, expect to see a diverse array of maps that push the boundaries of Brickadia Deathrun.

From sprawling landscapes to intricate urban mazes, each map is crafted with passion and attention to detail, promising endless hours of thrilling gameplay.
