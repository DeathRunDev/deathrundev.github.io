DevBlog #1 Developing
Brickadias Deathrun - Part 2

Creating the lobby, shops, minigame, rules, and many other necessary features!

Red
January 30th 2024

Welcome to an exclusive behind-the-scenes look at the development journey of Brickadia Deathrun, where we take you through the meticulous process of crafting every element that brings our game to life.

Crafting the Surrounding Mountains: Majestic Backdrop

Our journey begins with the creation of the surrounding mountains that encircle the lobby. Using advanced terrain editing tools, we sculpted naturalistic contours and peaks to establish a breathtaking backdrop that sets the tone for the immersive experience of Brickadia Deathrun.

Establishing the Staff HQ: Operational Command Center

Next, we erected the Staff HQ—a central command center where our dedicated team orchestrates events, manages the server, and ensures a smooth and enjoyable gameplay experience for all participants. This behind-the-scenes hub is essential for maintaining the seamless operation of our game world.

Creating the Waterfall and Pond: Tranquil Oasis

The cascading waterfall and tranquil pond were meticulously designed to enhance the lobby's ambiance. Dynamic water effects and ambient sounds transport players into a serene natural landscape, providing a peaceful escape within the adrenaline-fueled world of Deathrun.

Constructing Pathways, Park, and Center Spawn Area: Navigational Hub

Interconnected pathways wind through the lobby, guiding players to various locales such as the park area and center spawn. The design emphasizes accessibility and exploration, encouraging players to discover hidden treasures and prepare for their Deathrun adventures.

Introducing the Deathrun Portal: Gateway to Adventure

At the heart of the lobby lies the Deathrun Portal—a beckoning gateway that transports players into heart-pounding gameplay. Step through to embark on thrilling challenges and adrenaline-fueled races against death itself.

Establishing the Ability Shop and Weapon Shop: Strategic Arsenal

The Ability Shop and Weapon Shop offer players essential resources for survival in the Deathrun arena. Choose from a wide array of abilities and weaponry to customize your loadout and maximize your chances of success.

Creating the Leaderboard Zone: Celebrating Achievements

The Leaderboard Zone showcases the achievements of top players, providing inspiration and motivation to climb the ranks and earn your place among the elite.

Unveiling Deathrun Diner: Social Hub and Strategy Center

Deathrun Diner serves as a vibrant social hub where players can strategize, relax, and forge friendships before and after intense Deathrun matches. Its retro-inspired design fosters camaraderie and community engagement, enriching the overall gameplay experience.

Constructing the Big Tree with Parkour Challenges: Vertical Exploration

A towering tree stands as a centerpiece, surrounded by exhilarating parkour challenges that test players' agility and skill. Scale its branches to unlock rewards and showcase your mastery of movement.

Explore and Discover More!

Beyond these central features, there's a wealth of hidden gems and surprises awaiting exploration. Dive deeper into the world of Brickadia Deathrun and uncover its secrets as you embark on an unforgettable gaming adventure.

Planning for Future Features: Theoretical Framework

As we continue to expand and refine Brickadia Deathrun, we envision exciting future features such as:

XP System, Levels, and Leaderboards

Prepare to embark on a progression journey with our XP system. Earn experience points (XP) through gameplay activities, level up to unlock rewards and abilities, and climb the leaderboards to showcase your skills.

XP System Mathematics and Behavior

The XP system operates on a mathematical model where specific actions and achievements within the game contribute to XP gain. Each level requires a certain amount of XP to progress, creating a sense of accomplishment and motivating players to engage with various aspects of the game.

Deathrun Portal: Gateway to Adventure

The Deathrun Portal stands as a beckoning gateway to adrenaline-pumping gameplay. Step through to join Deathrun matches seamlessly, immersing yourself in thrilling challenges and heart-racing excitement.

Ability System: Unleash Your Potential

Unlock and customize powerful abilities through our dynamic Ability System. From enhanced speed to special attacks, abilities add strategic depth and variety to your Deathrun experience, allowing you to tailor your playstyle and outwit your opponents.

Shop System: Theoretical Implementation

Our shop system is designed with efficiency and player convenience in mind. Using optimized code, players can browse, purchase, and equip items seamlessly within the game interface. The system supports a wide range of items, from weapons and abilities to cosmetic upgrades, fostering player choice and customization.

Embark with us on this creative journey as we continue to push the boundaries of immersive multiplayer gaming. Stay tuned for more updates and insights as we unveil new features and enhancements. Together, let's redefine what it means to experience the thrill of Deathrun.
