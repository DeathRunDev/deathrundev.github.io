Brickadias Deathrun - Part 1
You've seen Deathrun in many games before. It's time for an upgrade. What could it be?

In our inaugural devblog, we embark on an exciting journey into the development realm of Brickadia's Deathrun. Delving deep into the intricate process of crafting adrenaline-pumping experiences, we unveil the initial steps taken to bring this thrilling game mode to life. From conceptualization to implementation, join us as we share insights, challenges, and triumphs encountered along the path of creating the inaugural Deathrun maps. Stay tuned for an exclusive behind-the-scenes glimpse into the making of Brickadia's Deathrun, where innovation and imagination collide to redefine the boundaries of excitement.
